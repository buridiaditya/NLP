\textit{\underline{SUB PART 1 - Zipf's law verification}}\\
\vspace{3mm}
From Zipf's law : $ f\propto \frac{1}{r}$\\
$ f*r = constant $ \\
$ \log f + \log r = constant $ \\
$ \log f = -\log r + constant $ \\
